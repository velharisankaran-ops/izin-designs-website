Process

A clear path from brief to finished space.

  1. Brief

    Understand site, lifestyle, budget, timelines, and the mood the space should carry.

  2. Concept

    Develop layouts, references, material direction, finishes, and key spatial decisions.

  3. Detail

    Prepare drawings, furniture details, lighting plans, BOQs, and vendor-ready specifications.

  4. Execute

    Coordinate production, procurement, site progress, styling, snag checks, and handover.
